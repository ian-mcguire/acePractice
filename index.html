Follow journalist Jake Adelstein as he navigates the perilous world of Tokyo's Yakuza. Set in the late 1990s, Tokyo Vice delves into the gritty realities of crime and corruption in Tokyo. The show offers a gripping, immersive look at the complexities of journalism and organized crime in Japan.
